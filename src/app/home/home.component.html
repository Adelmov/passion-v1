Carlos Santana

My idol, My inspiration.

By Gabriel Conde

Carlos Santana is a guitar legend. Delivered with a level of passion and soul equal to the legendary sonic charge of his guitar, the sound of Carlos Santana is one of the world’s best-known musicians. For more than four decades-from Santana’s earliest days as a groundbreaking Afro-Latin-blues-rock fusion outfit in San Francisco-Carlos has been the visionary force behind artistry that transcends musical genres and generational, cultural and geographical boundaries. Ever since I was exposed to Santana and his perspective on music, my passion for guitar only grew stronger. I loved grabbing my stratocaster and play songs like Smooth.

Check out my cover of Smooth by Santana where I solo over a backing track and let my fingers do the magic.
